About Us

Hostel 2 is one of the most vibrant and dynamic hostels of IIT Bombay. Known for its rich history and enthusiastic residents, we celebrate diversity, innovation, and academic excellence. It's more than just a hostel; it's a place where lifelong memories are made, friendships are built, and creativity thrives. The attitude here is one of relentless determination, where the spirit of never losing propels us forward. This vibrant community embraces challenges head-on, fostering an environment of growth and excellence. With a plethora of talents and aspirations, the residents of Hostel 2 embody the true meaning of being wild at heart. It is this very spirit that sets us apart, making Hostel 2 the epitome of camaraderie and the crown jewel of IIT-Bombay's hostels.
